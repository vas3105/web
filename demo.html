Founded in 2019, Loop MIT is the Official Hyperloop Student Project Team of MIT, Manipal

We are a group of highly motivated students looking to make the fifth mode of transportation a reality! With a relentless focus on sustainability, speed, and safety, we are contributing towards developing high-speed, sustainable mobility solutions.

We have cleared the first rounds of the prestigious European Hyperloop Week (EHW) in the years 2022 and 2023, which were held in Europe.

Currently making our prototype hyperloop pod, we aim to revolutionize global transportation.

Electrical

The electronics and electrical subsystem is responsible for managing power, using a Battery Management System (BMS), controlling various systems, using different sensors and ensuring effective connectivity between different parts of the pod. Also, our Hyperloop communication system is centered around Raspberry Pi and ESP32, enabling real-time updates through UART, Node.js, and Socket.io fused with wireless technology and antennas to enhance reliability.

Levitation and Propulsion

The propulsion system is crucial for achieving the unprecedented speeds and efficiency associated with this cutting-edge transportation concept. We use a meticulously engineered single-sided linear induction motor, which is the driving force behind propelling the pod through a partial vacuum tube at high speeds. The levitation department is responsible for maintaining the vehicle's elevation above the track via magnetic levitation to lift the pod and keep it suspended during travel.

Mechanical

The mechanical subsystem of the hyperloop pod plays a fundamental role in ensuring the overall functionality, safety, and performance of the vehicle. It encompasses a variety of components like the aeroshell, chassis, braking system, lateral control module, and vertical stability system. Together, these components work harmoniously to create a reliable and safe mechanical infrastructure, contributing to the vehicle's optimal operation in its intended environment within a hyperloop system.

Management

The management subsystem plays a crucial role in overseeing and coordinating the other subsystems while ensuring that the team's objectives are met successfully on time, within budget via an organized and coordinated endeavor. Our Graphic designers infuse creativity and visual appeal into the team's identity while the Web Developers contribute their technical prowess to maintain an engaging and user-friendly online presence, showcasing the team's achievements and enhancing accessibility.
